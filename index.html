Shadow's Profile

Biography

Shadow is 4 year old British Shorthair cat who is very playful. He enjoys taking regular naps and trying to eat my plants. Shadow was adopted by his owners when he was 8 months old and has tripled in size!

Shadow once went on a holiday to the see side. He stayed in a caravan and really enjoyed watching the birds. But the drive there was about 2 hours and Shadow meowed throughout the whole journey.

Currently, Shadow is snoozing in his little cat-bed below Alicia's desk.
